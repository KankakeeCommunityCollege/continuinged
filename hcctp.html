Highway Construction Careers Training Program

12-Week FREE Program!

This is a twelve-week training program that helps you find a career path into union construction apprenticeships. The first six weeks we will be in a classroom learning about math, Flaggers, Forklift, Heavy Equipment Training, CPR, Speech, Soft Skills, Print Reading and learning about all the construction trades (Not just Highway Construction). The other six week of the class is doing hands-on training, like concreate, Welding, Mock house framing, Electrical, Roofing, every class is ran differently depending on the trades that are taking applications. Our goal is for you to have the opportunity for a career with high wage potential. Learn the skills necessary for acceptance into the construction trades.

In addition to our free education program you will receive:

  • A stipend of $10 an hour for every hour you complete
  • A voucher to Redwing Boots to use during your training
  • $350 tool bag
  • $150 in welding equipment
  • All PPE that you need to be successful in your career path into construction

Requirements:

  • Valid driver's license
  • 18 years or older
  • High School diploma/GED
  • Drug Test (we test for THC)
  • Interest working in the Construction Trades
