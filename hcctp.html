Highway Construction Careers Training Program

12-Week FREE Program!

Our goal is for you to have the opportunity for a career with high wage potential. Learn the skills necessary for acceptance into the highway construction and related trades.

Requirements:

  • Valid driver's license
  • 18 years or older
  • High School diploma/GED
  • Drug Test (we test for THC)
  • Interest working in the Construction Trades

  • Wednesday, July 19, 9 a.m. - 12 p.m.
  • Thursday July 20, 9 a.m. - 12 p.m., and 5:30 p.m. - 9:30 p.m.
