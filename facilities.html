KCC offers a welcoming and beautiful space for your next training, workshop, conference, meeting, or special event. You can host any event here, from an informal lunch to a full-weekend conference with over 200 participants.

KCC offers modern amenities, versatile rooms, and a helpful staff to ensure a successful event.

Facility Use Request Form

Summary of Facilities

  • Conveniently located near Interstate 57, Exit 308.
  • Plentiful free parking.
  • Guest Wi-Fi throughout the building and campus.
  • State-of-the-art presentation systems - with technology assistance available.
  • More than 20 meeting rooms available. Capacities of 10-50.
  • Two auditoriums, with capacities of 100 and 240.
  • On-site catering, for everything from continental breakfasts to sit-down dinners. (Catering Guide)

Individual Rooms

Computer Labs

  • Computer lab with the latest software.
  • Seating capacities - 25.

Conference Hall

The two story conference hall is an ideal professional setting for large lectures or keynote speakers.

Amenities:

  • Plugs at seats.
  • Big screen.
  • Sound system.
  • Technology for presentations.
  • Seating capacity - 100.

Iroquois Room

  • Big screen.
  • Sound system.
  • Technology for presentations.
  • Attached Kitchenette for easy and convenient buffet serving.
  • Seating capacity - 120.

Auditorium

  • Full-size movie screen with projector, and capability for computer-generated presentations.
  • Presentations equipment includes a Windows 11 computer with Microsoft Office, VGA/Audio ports for laptop computers, and a document camera.
  • Seating capacity - 240.

Governor's Room - D300

  • Easily accessible by stairs or elevator.
  • Large private room with comfortable, moveable seating and tables.
  • Video conferencing technology for virtual meetings.
  • Smart technology for presentations.
  • Sink located in the room.
  • Restrooms conveniently located outside of room.
  • Seating capacity - 16.

We would be happy to answer questions regarding your facility rental needs. Please contact Erica Billington, coordinator, Continuing Education and Business Partnerships, ebillington@kcc.edu.
