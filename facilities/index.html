We can accommodate your next training event. Our conference facilities include state-of-the-art presentation systems for any event from an informal lunch to a full-weekend conference for 400 participants. 

  • The 250-seat auditorium is outfitted with a full-size movie screen, projector, and capability for computer-generated presentations. Presentation equipment includes a Windows 7 computer with Microsoft Office, VGA/Audio ports for laptop computers, a document camera, and a DVD/CD player.
  • More than 20 other meeting rooms have capacities of 10 to 50.
  • The in-house food service can cater everything from continental breakfasts to sit-down dinners.
  • The two-story conference hall seats 100 and is an ideal professional setting for large lectures or keynote speakers.
  • Multi-purpose classroom.
  • Two 20 seat computer labs with the latest software.

  • Two 34 seat training rooms.

  • The Iroquois Room seats 120 and offers an attached catering service room.
